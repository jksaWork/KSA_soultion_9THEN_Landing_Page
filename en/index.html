Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum initial investment amount is 1000 SAR. After that, the customer can add any additional amount without a minimum.

What are the types of investments that we offer?

Values of various types invest in various assets locally and globally, such as Saudi and international stocks, real estate, and sukuk. We believe in the effectiveness of long-term investment at low costs, and therefore we rely mainly on passive investment tools such as Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs), as scientific studies have found their effectiveness and superiority over active investment over time.

Are the investments compatible with Sharia provisions?

Yes, all of our investment products have been reviewed and approved by the Sharia Committee.

What are the fees charged for the service?

The financial fee is 1% of the funds managed for the client in the platform on an annual basis and is deducted on a monthly basis.

Can I deposit and withdraw easily?

Yes, through the platform, you can deposit and withdraw investments at any time. Knowing that the process may take from 2 to 5 working days.
